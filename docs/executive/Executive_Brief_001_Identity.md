# Executive Strategy Brief: Package 001
## Identity Control Plane Defense: Securing the New Perimeter

**Target Audience:** CISO, Board of Directors, Risk Committee
**Strategic Alignment:** ISO 27001 (A.9), NIST CSF (PR.AC), GDPR (Art. 32)

### 1. The Strategic Threat: Identity-Led Intrusions
In 2025-2026, the "Network Perimeter" has effectively vanished. Identity is now the primary attack surface. Threat actors (e.g., Scattered Spider, APT29) no longer "break in"—they "log in" using stolen session tokens, bypassed MFA, and abused Privileged Identity Management (PIM).

### 2. Business Risk & Impact
*   **Financial:** Average cost of an identity-based breach exceeds $4.8M.
*   **Operational:** Unauthorized Global Admin access can lead to a total tenant wipeout in under 10 minutes.
*   **Regulatory:** Non-compliance with MFA mandates (DORA/SEC) leads to heavy fines and loss of digital trust.

### 3. The SOaC Advantage: Distributed Intelligence
*   **Body (Detection):** Moves beyond static "failed logins" to detect **Token Theft** and **PIM Abuse** in real-time.
*   **Brain (Reasoning):** AI analyzes if a "Global Admin" activation matches the user's historical behavior and current geolocation.
*   **Purpose (Response):** Automated "Kill-Switch" that revokes all active sessions across M365, AWS, and Salesforce simultaneously.

### 4. ROI & Resilience Metrics
*   **MTTR Reduction:** Automated identity containment reduces response time from hours to <30 seconds.
*   **Efficiency:** AI-driven "Brain" filtering reduces false-positive identity alerts by 65%.
